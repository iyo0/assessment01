About BPSU and my program

Bataan Peninsula State University (BPSU) is a public state university situated in the province of Bataan, Philippines. BPSU is renowned for offering accessible and quality education to students coming from diverse backgrounds, particularly those who aspire to gain college education without leaving the province. Being a state university, the primary goal of BPSU is to produce graduates who are not only competent in their chosen careers, but also equipped to serve the community and contribute to the development of the country.

BPSU has several campuses spread all over the province of Bataan, making education more accessible to students from various municipalities. Each campus offers different programs, giving students the opportunity to choose a strand that suits their interests. With this in mind, BPSU becomes a central learning hub in the province and supports the growth of the province by producing educated and employable individuals.

The university is also concerned with learning and training. Many courses in BPSU involve laboratory work, practicals, internships, and field exposure so that students can have a feel of the application of what they have learned. This is a very important approach since it helps students become more confident and work-ready after graduation.

Apart from academics, BPSU also encourages student development in terms of organizations, leadership, and campus activities. Students are given the opportunity to engage in organizations that align with their interests, whether it is technology, sports, arts, or community service. This is essential for students as it helps them develop communication skills, teamwork, and leadership, which are also valuable in the workplace.
